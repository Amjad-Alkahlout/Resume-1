My Top 3 movies of all time

Extraction 3

"Extraction 3" is the third action-packed installment in the *Extraction* series, starring Chris Hemsworth as Tyler Rake, a skilled mercenary. Directed by Sam Hargrave, the film promises intense fight sequences, emotional depth, and high-stakes missions as Rake faces new challenges, continuing the franchise's legacy of gritty, adrenaline-fueled storytelling.

Bad Boys: Ride or Die

"Bad Boys: Ride or Die" (2024) is the highly anticipated fourth installment in the Bad Boys franchise, reuniting Will Smith as Mike Lowrey and Martin Lawrence as Marcus Burnett. Directed by Adil El Arbi and Bilall Fallah, who also helmed Bad Boys for Life (2020), the film promises to deliver the series' trademark mix of explosive action, sharp humor, and the dynamic chemistry between the two leads.

The Fast & Furious

franchise is a high-octane action series centered around fast cars, heists, and the theme of family. Led by Dominic Toretto (Vin Diesel) and Brian O'Conner (Paul Walker), the films evolved from street racing to globe-trotting missions filled with over-the-top stunts, explosive action, and emotional moments. Known for its diverse cast, iconic cars, and themes of loyalty and redemption, the franchise has become a global phenomenon, with 10 main films (as of 2023) and spin-offs like Hobbs & Shaw. The saga continues to expand, blending adrenaline-pumping action with heartfelt storytelling.
